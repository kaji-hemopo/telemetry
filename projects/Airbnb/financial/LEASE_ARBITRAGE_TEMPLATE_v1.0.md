## 📈 LEASE ARBITRAGE ADVANTAGES

### **vs Purchase Model:**
```
METRIC           | LEASE ARBITRAGE | PURCHASE
Capital Required | ¥300K-¥500K     | ¥19M-¥30M
ROI Potential    | 100%+ annual    | 2-5% annual
Break-even       | 3-6 months      | 2-4 years
Risk Level       | Lower           | Higher
Scalability      | Faster          | Slower
Flexibility      | Higher          | Lower
```

### **Key Benefits:**
1. **Lower Capital:** Accessible with ¥300,000-¥500,000
2. **Higher ROI:** Returns measured in months, not years
3. **Test & Learn:** Can test multiple locations/markets
4. **Lower Risk:** Less capital tied up in each property
5. **Faster Scaling:** Add properties more quickly
6. **Market Flexibility:** Adjust to demand changes

## 🎯 TARGET PROPERTY PROFILE

### **Ideal Lease Property:**
- **Location:** Within 2-hour drive from Chigasaki
- **Rent:** ¥80,000 - ¥150,000/month
- **Size:** 1-2 bedrooms, 30-60m²
- **Condition:** Good to excellent, minimal upgrades needed
- **Landlord:** Open to minpaku, good communication
- **Lease Terms:** 2+ years, reasonable break clauses
- **Amenities:** Basic furniture/appliances included
- **Transport:** Walkable to station/attractions

### **Priority Areas for Lease:**
1. **Chigasaki Local:** Maximum hands-on, local knowledge
2. **Kamakura Tourist:** High demand, may have experience
3. **Yokohama Urban:** Steady demand, professional landlords
4. **Shonan Beach:** Seasonal premium, year-round base
5. **Atami Resort:** Premium pricing, specialized market

## ⚠️ RISK ASSESSMENT (LEASE SPECIFIC)

### **Unique Lease Risks:**
1. **Landlord Issues:** Changing mind about minpaku
2. **Lease Termination:** Early termination by landlord
3. **Rent Increases:** Significant rent hikes at renewal
4. **Regulatory Changes:** Minpaku regulations tightening
5. **Neighbor Complaints:** Causing lease problems
6. **Property Damage:** Deposit disputes

### **Mitigation Strategies:**
1. **Clear Lease Agreement:** Minpaku rights in writing
2. **Good Relationship:** Regular communication with landlord
3. **Quality Management:** Minimize neighbor complaints
4. **Insurance:** Proper coverage for damages
5. **Legal Review:** Have lease reviewed by expert
6. **Exit Planning:** Clear strategy for lease end
