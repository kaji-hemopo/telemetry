## ⚙️ OPERATIONAL PROCESSES

### **Guest Inquiry Response System**
**Response Time Target:** <1 hour during business hours (9 AM - 9 PM)
**Automated Responses:** For common questions (pricing, availability, amenities)
**Template Library:** 20+ pre-written responses for common scenarios

### **Booking Management Workflow**
```
1. Booking Received → Auto-confirm if meets criteria
2. Send welcome email with house rules
3. Add to calendar (Airbnb + Google Calendar sync)
4. Schedule pre-arrival communications
5. Prepare cleaning schedule
6. Update access code if needed
7. Confirm with guest 24h before arrival
```

### **Issue Resolution Protocol**
**Tier 1 Issues (Guest can resolve):**
- Wi-Fi not working (reboot instructions)
- Appliance not working (basic troubleshooting)
- Lost item (guidance for retrieval)

**Tier 2 Issues (Require intervention):**
- Locked out
- Major appliance failure
- Heating/cooling issues
- Plumbing problems

**Tier 3 Issues (Emergency):**
- Fire, flood, gas leak
- Medical emergency
- Security breach

**Response Time Targets:**
- Tier 1: <30 minutes
- Tier 2: <2 hours  
- Tier 3: Immediate

### **Review Management System**
**Post-Stay Review Process:**
1. Automated review request 2 hours after check-out
2. Monitor for new reviews daily
3. Respond to all reviews within 24 hours
4. Address negative feedback immediately
5. Incorporate feedback into system improvements

**Review Response Templates:**
- Positive reviews: Thank you + personal touch
- Constructive feedback: Acknowledge + commit to improvement
- Negative reviews: Apologize + offer solution + take offline
