## 1. MISSION
**Validate the Titan Fit business concept:** Premium Western-sized basics for tall/heavy individuals in Japan.

**Timeline:** 6-week deep research
**Budget:** Research phase only - no inventory costs
**Deliverable:** Go/No-Go recommendation with confidence score

---

## 2. BACKGROUND
**Problem:** Western-sized individuals (especially tall/heavy builds) in Japan struggle to find properly fitting clothing. Japanese sizing runs small, and importing from Western countries is expensive/slow.

**Personal Validation:** Jackson (Empire Lead) is 6'4", 112kg and experiences this problem firsthand.

**Existing Asset:** We have a Next.js landing page blueprint ready for deployment.

---

## 3. RESEARCH PHASES

### PHASE 1: Market Validation (Week 1-2)
**Objective:** Quantify market size and validate problem severity.

**Tasks:**
1. **Search Trend Analysis**
   - Google Trends for "big and tall Japan", "oversized clothing Japan"
   - Keyword search volume estimates
   - Seasonal patterns in search behavior

2. **Competitor Landscape Mapping**
   - Map ALL competitors: Uniqlo, ASOS, Amazon Japan, specialty importers
   - Document: Pricing, sizing ranges, shipping costs, delivery times
   - Identify gaps in current offerings

3. **Online Community Analysis**
   - r/japanlife - Search for clothing/sizing discussions
   - Facebook expat groups in Japan
   - Japan-based forums (GaijinPot, etc.)
   - Document: Frequency of complaints, specific pain points

4. **Market Size Estimation**
   - Japan Ministry of Justice: Foreign resident statistics by nationality
   - Height/weight distributions for Western populations in Japan
   - Apparel market size data (Japan Apparel Industry Association)

5. **Social Media Listening**
   - Instagram/Twitter: #JapanProblems, #clothingJapan, expat hashtags
   - Document: Visual evidence of fit issues, brand mentions

**Deliverable:** Phase 1 Report with quantified market size and problem validation.

---

### PHASE 2: Solution Validation (Week 3-4)
**Objective:** Test pricing and collect waitlist.

**Tasks:**
1. **Landing Page Launch**
   - Deploy existing Titan Fit blueprint to Vercel (free tier)
   - Configure email collection (ConvertKit/Mailchimp free tier)

2. **Waitlist Campaign**
   - Target: 500+ email signups
   - Channels: Expat forums, Facebook groups, relevant subreddits
   - Offer: Early access + potential discount

3. **Price Sensitivity Testing**
   - Create 3 landing page variants with different price points
   - Test: ¥3,000 vs ¥4,000 vs ¥5,000 for basic t-shirt
   - Measure: Click-through rates, email signup rates

4. **Supplier Research**
   - Identify 10+ potential manufacturers in Vietnam/China
   - Criteria: Japan export experience, MOQ ≤100 units, sample availability
   - Document: Lead times, pricing, quality samples available

5. **Logistics Cost Modeling**
   - Yamato/Japan Post shipping rates (domestic Japan)
   - 3PL provider costs in Tokyo/Osaka
   - Import duty calculations for clothing

**Deliverable:** Phase 2 Report with waitlist results, price sensitivity data, and vetted supplier list.

---

### PHASE 3: Feasibility Study (Week 5-6)
**Objective:** Complete business model and risk assessment.

**Tasks:**
1. **Financial Model Creation**
   - Unit economics: CAC, LTV, margins
   - 12-month cash flow projection
   - Break-even analysis
   - Investment required vs. return timeline

2. **Legal Structure Research**
   - KK vs branch office for foreign entrepreneurs
   - Import/export regulations for clothing
   - Consumer protection laws in Japan
   - Tax implications

3. **Operations Planning**
   - Inventory management system requirements
   - Customer service workflow
   - Returns/exchanges policy framework
   - Quality control processes

4. **Risk Assessment Matrix**
   - Probability/impact analysis of key risks
   - Mitigation strategies for each high-risk area
   - Contingency planning

5. **Automation Audit**
   - Identify processes that can be automated from Day 1
   - Tools/software requirements
   - Implementation timeline

**Deliverable:** Complete Feasibility Report with Go/No-Go recommendation.

## 4. CRITICAL QUESTIONS TO ANSWER

### Market Questions:
1. How many Western expats in Japan are tall/heavy enough to need special sizing?
2. What is their average spend on clothing annually?
3. How severe is the pain point? (1-10 scale based on online discussions)
4. What are the most requested clothing items?

### Business Questions:
1. What price premium will customers pay for proper fit?
2. What are realistic customer acquisition costs in Japan?
3. Can we achieve 40%+ gross margins?
4. What is the minimum viable inventory investment?

### Risk Questions:
1. What are the top 3 risks that could kill this business?
2. What regulatory hurdles exist for clothing imports?
3. How reliable are Asian manufacturers for consistent sizing?

---

## 5. KILL CRITERIA
**Stop the project if ANY of these occur:**

1. **Waitlist Failure:** <500 email signups after 4 weeks of marketing
2. **Price Resistance:** >80% of waitlist indicate unwillingness to pay 20% premium
3. **Supplier Issues:** No manufacturer found with ≤100 MOQ and Japan experience
4. **Margin Compression:** Projected gross margins <30% after all costs
5. **Market Size:** Addressable market <10,000 potential customers in Japan
