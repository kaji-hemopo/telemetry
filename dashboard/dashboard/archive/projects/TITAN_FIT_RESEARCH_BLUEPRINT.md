## 2. PROBLEM DEFINITION
### 2.1 Core Problem Statement
Western-sized individuals (especially tall/heavy builds) living in Japan face significant challenges finding clothing that fits properly. Japanese sizing standards are smaller than Western equivalents, leading to:
- Sleeves/pants that are too short
- Shoulders/chest that are too tight
- Limited selection in local stores
- High shipping costs and import duties for Western brands
- Long delivery times for international orders
- Cultural mismatch in style preferences

### 2.2 Problem Validation Sources
- [x] Personal experience (Empire member: Jackson, 6'4", 112kg)
- [ ] Customer interviews (target: 20+ tall/heavy expats in Japan)
- [ ] Market research reports (Japan apparel market)
- [ ] Online community discussions (r/japanlife, Facebook expat groups)
- [ ] Search volume data ("big and tall Japan", "oversized clothing Japan")
- [ ] Competitor analysis (existing solutions)

### 2.3 Problem Severity Metrics
- **Frequency:** Monthly (clothing is recurring need)
- **Pain Level:** 8/10 (affects daily comfort and professional appearance)
- **Current Workarounds:** Import from home countries, tailor alterations, settle for ill-fitting clothes
- **Willingness to Pay:** Estimated 20-30% premium over standard pricing for proper fit

## 5. SOLUTION ARCHITECTURE
### 5.1 Product/Service Description
Curated collection of oversized basics (t-shirts, hoodies, jeans, polos) designed for tall/heavy builds, with:
- Extended sizes (XL-5XL, tall lengths)
- Japanese domestic shipping (1-3 days)
- Bilingual customer service
- Fit guarantee/returns
- Community-driven product development

### 5.2 Key Features

2. **Fit Gallery** - Real customer photos by size
