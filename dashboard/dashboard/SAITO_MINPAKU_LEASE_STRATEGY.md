## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The **Minpaku lease arbitrage model** (サブリース) eliminates property purchase risk while maintaining Minpaku regulatory advantages. You **lease apartments long-term**, obtain Minpaku licenses, then **sub-lease short-term** at premium rates. 

**Key Advantage:** **¥0 property purchase capital** with **¥500,000-¥1,000,000/month potential** from 3-5 leased units. This is the **fastest path to Minpaku revenue** with **minimal financial risk**.

**Recommended Starting Point:** **Fukuoka 1-bedroom apartments** near Hakata Station. Target: **3 leased units within 90 days**.

**Step 3:** Sub-lease short-term (¥4,000-¥6,000/night, 65-75% occupancy)  
**Step 4:** Profit from spread: **¥100,000-¥200,000/month/unit**

## 5. REGULATORY STRATEGY: LEASE-SPECIFIC

### Key Difference from Ownership:
- **Landlord Approval Required:** Critical path item
- **Building Management Approval:** Often harder than municipality
- **Insurance Complexity:** Need both tenant + Minpaku coverage
- **Exit Planning:** Must consider lease end dates

### Step-by-Step Licensing Process:

**Step 1: Landlord Education**
- Prepare "Minpaku Benefits Package" for landlords
- Include: Higher rent, stable income, professional management
- Address concerns: Insurance, neighbor issues, wear-and-tear

**Step 2: Building Management**
- Research building's Minpaku policy (ask current residents)
- If no policy: Propose trial period (3-6 months)
- Offer to handle all neighbor communications

**Step 3: Municipal Application**
- Submit standard Minpaku application
- Include: Landlord approval letter, building permission
- Timeline: 30-60 days approval in Fukuoka

**Step 4: Operations Setup**
- Hire local manager (required by law)
- Install safety equipment (if needed)
- Set up compliance systems
- Create emergency procedures

### Risk Mitigation:

**Landlord Risk:**  
- **Mitigation:** 2-year lease minimum, renewal options
- **Mitigation:** Personal guarantee limited to deposit
- **Mitigation:** Exit plan if Minpaku fails (convert to traditional rental)

**Regulatory Risk:**  
- **Mitigation:** Only pursue buildings with existing Minpaku
- **Mitigation:** Maintain perfect compliance records
- **Mitigation:** Regular communication with municipality

**Operational Risk:**  
- **Mitigation:** Redundant manager coverage
- **Mitigation:** 3-month operating reserve per unit
- **Mitigation:** Comprehensive insurance coverage
