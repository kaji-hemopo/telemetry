## 🔢 CALCULATION FORMULAS

### **Key Formulas:**
1. **Monthly Revenue:** = ADR × Occupancy Rate × 30 days
2. **Monthly Profit:** = Monthly Revenue - Total Monthly Costs
3. **Cash-on-Cash Return:** = (Annual Profit / Total Investment) × 100%
4. **Break-even Months:** = Total Investment / Monthly Profit
5. **Profit Margin:** = (Monthly Profit / Monthly Revenue) × 100%

### **Assumption Guidelines:**
1. **Occupancy Year 1:** Start conservative, increase monthly
2. **ADR Growth:** 3-5% annual increase assumed
3. **Cost Inflation:** 2-3% annual increase
4. **Vacancy Buffer:** 5-10% below target occupancy
5. **Maintenance Reserve:** 5-10% of monthly revenue
