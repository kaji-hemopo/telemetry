## 🔍 RESEARCH UPDATE - 22:36 JST

### **Key Findings:**
1. **National Framework:** Japan's "Private Lodging Business Act" (住宅宿泊事業法) establishes basic rules
2. **180-Day Limit:** Maximum operating days per year (can be less in some areas)
3. **Notification System:** Must notify local government, not a "permission" system
4. **Safety Requirements:** Fire safety equipment, emergency instructions, guest registration

### **Fukuoka-Specific Information Found:**
- **Notification Office:** Fukuoka City Tourism, Culture and Sports Bureau (観光・文化・スポーツ局)
- **Department:** MICE・民泊推進室 (MICE & Minpaku Promotion Office)
- **Address:** 福岡市中央区天神1-8-1 福岡市役所
- **Phone:** 092-711-4111 (Fukuoka City Hall main line)
- **Process:** Submit notification form with required documents
- **Timeline:** Typically 2-4 weeks for processing
- **Fees:** Notification fee approximately ¥10,000-¥20,000

1. **住宅宿泊事業届出書** (Private Lodging Business Notification Form)
2. **添付書類一覧** (List of Required Attachments)
