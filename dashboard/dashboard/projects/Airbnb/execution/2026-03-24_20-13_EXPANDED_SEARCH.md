## 💰 FINANCIAL ANALYSIS FRAMEWORK

### **Data Collection for Each Property:**
1. **Purchase Price:** List price + estimated negotiation range
2. **Setup Costs:** Furniture, decor, photography, licensing
3. **Monthly Costs:** Mortgage, utilities, insurance, taxes
4. **Operational Costs:** Cleaning, maintenance, supplies
5. **Revenue Potential:** Based on comparable listings in area
6. **Occupancy Projection:** Market demand analysis
7. **Profit Calculation:** Revenue - all costs

### **Financial Metrics to Calculate:**
1. **Monthly Revenue:** ADR × Occupancy × 30 days
2. **Monthly Expenses:** Fixed + variable costs
3. **Monthly Profit:** Revenue - Expenses
4. **Cash-on-Cash Return:** Annual profit / Total investment
5. **Break-even Point:** Months to recover initial investment
6. **ROI:** Total return over investment period
7. **Cash Flow:** Monthly operating cash position
