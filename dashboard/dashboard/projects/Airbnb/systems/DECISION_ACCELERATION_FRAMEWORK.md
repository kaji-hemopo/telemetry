# Decision Acceleration Framework
**Created:** 2026-03-24 02:55 JST  
**Purpose:** Frameworks for rapid property decisions under Scenario C ultra-compression

## 🎯 FRAMEWORK OVERVIEW

### **Purpose:** Enable rapid property decisions for 8-day acquisition timeline
### **Scenario:** Scenario C (3-7 day delay) ultra-compressed execution
### **Challenge:** Maintain decision quality while compressing decision timeline from 7 days to 3 days

## 📋 INSTANT EVALUATION CRITERIA

### **Ultra-Compressed Decision Timeline:**
- **Standard Timeline:** 7 days for property evaluation and decision
- **Compressed Timeline:** 3 days for evaluation and decision (Day 3-5 of 8-day plan)
- **Compression:** 4-day reduction (57% compression)
- **Challenge:** Maintain quality while making decisions 2.3x faster

### **Instant Yes/No Criteria (5-Minute Initial Screening):**

#### **Mandatory Yes Criteria (ALL Must Be Yes):**
1. **Minpaku Allowance:** Written or confirmed verbal permission from landlord
2. **Location:** Within target areas (Chuo-ku, Hakata-ku, Minami-ku)
3. **Size:** 20-35m² (1R, 1K, or 1DK)
4. **Condition:** No major repairs needed (cosmetic only acceptable)
5. **Availability:** Can be occupied within 30 days
6. **Budget:** Rent ≤ ¥120,000/month (target ≤ ¥110,000)

#### **Instant No Criteria (Any One Eliminates Property):**
1. **Minpaku Prohibition:** Landlord explicitly prohibits minpaku
2. **Major Repairs:** Requires structural, plumbing, or electrical work
3. **Location Issues:** Outside target areas or poor access (>15 min walk to station)
4. **Size Issues:** <20m² (too small) or >35m² (too large/expensive)
5. **Availability Delay:** Cannot be occupied within 60 days
6. **Cost Exceedance:** Rent > ¥130,000/month (absolute maximum)

## ⚡ QUICK NEGOTIATION PROTOCOLS

### **Ultra-Compressed Negotiation Timeline:**
- **Standard:** 3 days for negotiation
- **Compressed:** 1.5 days for negotiation (Day 5-6.5 of 8-day plan)
- **Compression:** 50% reduction
- **Approach:** Simultaneous rather than sequential negotiation

### **Negotiation Priority Framework:**

#### **Tier 1: Non-Negotiable (Must Have)**
1. **Minpaku Permission:** Written confirmation
2. **Key Money Reduction:** Target 0-1 month (standard 1-2 months)
3. **Contract Terms:** Minimum 2-year contract with minpaku clause
4. **Move-in Date:** Within 30 days

#### **Tier 2: High Priority (Strongly Want)**
1. **Rent Reduction:** 5-10% below asking (if above ¥100,000)
2. **Deposit Reduction:** 1 month target (standard 1-2 months)
3. **Agency Fee Reduction:** 0.5-1 month target (standard 1 month)
4. **Early Access:** 1-2 weeks before contract for setup

#### **Tier 3: Nice to Have (Negotiable)**
1. **Rent Free Period:** 1-2 weeks for setup
2. **Minor Repairs:** Landlord covers cosmetic improvements
3. **Furniture Allowance:** Small allowance for essential furniture
4. **Utilities:** Inclusion of some utilities

### **Negotiation Strategy by Scenario:**

#### **Scenario A (Favorable Market):**
- **Approach:** Standard negotiation, reasonable requests
- **Concessions:** Willing to compromise on Tier 3 items
- **Timeline:** 2-3 days acceptable
- **Risk:** Moderate, maintain good relationship

#### **Scenario B (Competitive Market):**
- **Approach:** More aggressive, focus on Tier 1-2
- **Concessions:** Compromise on some Tier 2 items if needed
- **Timeline:** 1.5-2 days target
- **Risk:** Higher, may strain relationship

#### **Scenario C (Ultra-Compressed):**
- **Approach:** Maximum aggression, Tier 1 only
- **Concessions:** Minimal, walk away if Tier 1 not met
- **Timeline:** 1 day maximum
- **Risk:** High, relationship secondary to timeline

### **Walk-Away Criteria:**
1. **Minpaku Permission:** Not provided or uncertain
2. **Key Money:** >2 months (absolute maximum)
3. **Rent:** >¥120,000/month (absolute maximum)
4. **Contract Term:** <2 years or no minpaku clause
5. **Move-in Date:** >45 days from agreement

### **Emergency Concession Framework:**
- **Concession 1:** Accept higher key money (up to 2 months) if other terms excellent
- **Concession 2:** Accept slightly higher rent (up to ¥115,000) if location exceptional
- **Concession 3:** Accept shorter contract (18 months) if renewal likely
- **Concession 4:** Accept later move-in (up to 45 days) if parallel processing possible
- **Maximum Concessions:** No more than 2 emergency concessions per property

## ⚠️ RISK-ACCEPTANCE THRESHOLDS

### **Risk Categories for Compression Scenarios:**

#### **Category 1: Acceptable Risks (Always Acceptable)**
1. **Cosmetic Condition:** Minor repairs or updates needed
2. **Location Margin:** 10-15 minute walk to station (vs. 5-10 target)
3. **Rent Margin:** ¥110,001-¥115,000 (vs. ≤¥110,000 target)
4. **Landlord Neutral:** Not enthusiastic but permits minpaku
5. **Availability Margin:** 31-45 days (vs. ≤30 days target)

#### **Category 2: Moderate Risks (Acceptable under Compression)**
1. **Verbal Permission Only:** No written confirmation yet
2. **Location Compromise:** 15-20 minute walk to station
3. **Rent Compromise:** ¥115,001-¥120,000
4. **Condition Issues:** Minor functional repairs needed
5. **Availability Delay:** 46-60 days

#### **Category 3: High Risks (Acceptable only under Extreme Compression)**
1. **Permission Uncertainty:** Landlord "likely" to allow minpaku
2. **Location Issues:** >20 minute walk or less desirable area
3. **Rent Issues:** >¥120,000 but ≤¥125,000
4. **Condition Problems:** Multiple repairs needed
5. **Availability Problems:** >60 days

#### **Category 4: Unacceptable Risks (Never Acceptable)**
1. **Permission Denied:** Landlord prohibits minpaku
2. **Location Failure:** Outside target areas or very poor access
3. **Rent Failure:** >¥125,000/month
4. **Condition Failure:** Major structural or system repairs
5. **Availability Failure:** >90 days or uncertain

### **Risk Acceptance by Compression Level:**

#### **Level 1 (Standard):**
- **Acceptable:** Category 1 only
- **Maximum Risk Score:** 2/10
- **Quality Focus:** Maximum quality, minimum risk

#### **Level 2 (Moderate Compression):**
- **Acceptable:** Category 1-2
- **Maximum Risk Score:** 4/10
- **Balance:** Quality with some timeline compromise

#### **Level 3 (Significant Compression - Scenario C):**
- **Acceptable:** Category 1-3
- **Maximum Risk Score:** 6/10
- **Focus:** Timeline achievement with managed risk

#### **Level 4 (Extreme Compression):**
- **Acceptable:** Category 1-3 with emergency concessions
- **Maximum Risk Score:** 8/10
- **Priority:** Timeline achievement above all else

#### **Level 5 (Maximum Compression):**
- **Acceptable:** Category 1-4 (except absolute prohibitions)
- **Maximum Risk Score:** 9/10
- **Emergency:** Timeline failure imminent, accept high risk

### **Risk Scoring System:**
- **Category 1 Risk:** 1 point each
- **Category 2 Risk:** 2 points each
- **Category 3 Risk:** 3 points each
- **Category 4 Risk:** Automatic rejection
- **Maximum by Level:** As defined above

### **Risk Mitigation for Accepted Risks:**
1. **Verbal Permission:** Get written confirmation as soon as possible
2. **Location Compromise:** Compensate with pricing or amenities
3. **Rent Compromise:** Negotiate harder on other terms
4. **Condition Issues:** Schedule repairs immediately after acquisition
5. **Availability Delay:** Use time for regulatory preparation

## 🚀 EMERGENCY APPROVAL PROCESSES

### **Standard Approval Process:**
- **Time:** 1-2 days for consideration and decision
- **Steps:** Evaluation → Analysis → Consultation → Decision
- **Consultation:** Input from team or advisors
- **Documentation:** Comprehensive decision documentation
- **Quality:** High confidence, low risk

### **Compressed Approval Process (Scenario C):**
- **Time:** 2-4 hours for decision
- **Steps:** Quick evaluation → Risk assessment → Immediate decision
- **Consultation:** Quick check with key advisor if available
- **Documentation:** Essential documentation only
- **Quality:** Moderate confidence, managed risk

### **Emergency Approval Process (Extreme Compression):**
- **Time:** 30-60 minutes for decision
- **Steps:** Instant criteria check → Go/No-go decision
- **Consultation:** Self-only decision if necessary
- **Documentation:** Minimal, post-decision documentation
- **Quality:** Lower confidence, higher risk accepted

### **Approval Authority Framework:**

#### **Level 1: Standard Authority**
- **Who:** Saito with team input
- **Time:** 1-2 days
- **Risk Limit:** Category 1 only
- **Consultation:** Required for Category 2+ risks
- **Documentation:** Full decision documentation

#### **Level 2: Compressed Authority**
- **Who:** Saito with quick advisor check
- **Time:** 2-4 hours
- **Risk Limit:** Category 1-2
- **Consultation:** Quick check if available
- **Documentation:** Essential points only

#### **Level 3: Emergency Authority**
- **Who:** Saito alone
- **Time:** 30-60 minutes
- **Risk Limit:** Category 1-3
- **Consultation:** Self-consultation only
- **Documentation:** Post-decision summary

#### **Level 4: Extreme Authority**
- **Who:** Saito with emergency override
- **Time:** 15-30 minutes
- **Risk Limit:** Category 1-4 (except absolute prohibitions)
- **Consultation:** None, immediate decision required
- **Documentation:** Minimal, may be retrospective

### **Decision Validation Post-Approval:**
1. **Immediate Validation:** Quick review of decision logic
2. **24-Hour Review:** Comprehensive review within 24 hours
3. **Team Review:** Team input after decision made
4. **Documentation Completion:** Full documentation within 48 hours
5. **Learning Capture:** Lessons for future decisions

### **Emergency Decision Triggers:**
1. **Timeline Critical:** Behind schedule by >24 hours
2. **Property Shortage:** No qualified properties identified
3. **Negotiation Stalled:** No progress after 24 hours
4. **Competitive Threat:** Another party interested in property
5. **Market Opportunity:** Exceptional property with short window
