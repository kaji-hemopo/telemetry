1. **Seasonal Trends:**
   - **Peak Season:** March-May (cherry blossoms, Golden Week), July-August (summer), October-November (autumn)

   - **Central Areas (Chuo-ku, Hakata-ku):** ¥8,000-¥12,000/night for 1R/1K

   - **Premium Locations:** Near Canal City, Tenjin: ¥10,000-¥15,000/night
   
3. **Occupancy Rates:**
   - **Annual Average:** 65-75% for well-managed properties
   - **Peak Months:** 85-95% occupancy
   - **Shoulder Months:** 70-80% occupancy
   - **Low Months:** 50-65% occupancy
   
4. **Competitor Analysis:**
   - **Direct Competitors:** 150-200 minpaku properties in central Fukuoka
   - **Quality Range:** Wide variation in quality and management
   - **Differentiation Opportunity:** Professional management, quality consistency, systems

#### **Property Type Analysis:**
1. **1R Apartments (20-25m²):**
   - **Advantages:** Lower rent, easier to furnish, popular with solo travelers
   - **Disadvantages:** Limited space, lower nightly rate potential
   - **Target Rent:** ¥80,000-¥95,000/month
   
2. **1K Apartments (25-30m²):**
   - **Advantages:** Separate kitchen, higher appeal, better rate potential
   - **Disadvantages:** Higher rent, slightly more furniture needed
   - **Target Rent:** ¥90,000-¥110,000/month
   
3. **1DK Apartments (30-35m²):**
   - **Advantages:** More space, dining area, higher rate potential
   - **Disadvantages:** Higher rent, more furniture, may exceed ideal size
