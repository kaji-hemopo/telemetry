## 🎯 SCORING SYSTEM

### **Total Score Interpretation:**
- **80-100 points:** EXCELLENT - High priority, move quickly
- **60-79 points:** GOOD - Strong candidate, proceed
- **40-59 points:** FAIR - Consider if no better options
- **<40 points:** POOR - Reject or renegotiate terms

### **Minimum Requirements:**
- Location score: ≥25/40
- Property score: ≥20/30  
- Financial score: ≥20/30
- Total score: ≥65/100

## 📝 EVALUATION CHECKLIST

### **Viewing Checklist:**
- [ ] Take photos of all rooms, kitchen, bathroom, entrance
- [ ] Test water pressure in shower and sinks
- [ ] Check electrical outlets in all rooms
- [ ] Inspect for mold, water damage, pests
- [ ] Test air conditioning/heating
- [ ] Check natural light at different times
- [ ] Assess noise level (street, neighbors)
- [ ] Verify storage space availability
- [ ] Check building amenities (elevator, security, laundry)
- [ ] Talk to neighbors/building manager if possible

### **Documentation Checklist:**
- [ ] Floor plan with measurements
- [ ] Photos of property condition
- [ ] Rental contract terms review
- [ ] Building rules regarding short-term rentals
- [ ] Utility setup requirements
- [ ] Internet installation options
- [ ] Parking availability (if needed)
- [ ] Garbage disposal rules
- [ ] Emergency contacts list

## 🔄 DECISION MATRIX

### **Green Light (Proceed):**
- Total score ≥70
- Monthly profit ≥¥80,000
- Startup cost ≤¥300,000
- Landlord approves minpaku
- No major renovation needed

### **Yellow Light (Negotiate):**
- Total score 60-69
- Monthly profit ¥60,000-¥79,999  
- Startup cost ¥300,000-¥350,000
- Landlord hesitant but negotiable
- Minor renovation needed (<¥100,000)

### **Red Light (Reject):**
- Total score <60
- Monthly profit <¥60,000
- Startup cost >¥350,000
- Landlord refuses minpaku
- Major renovation needed (>¥100,000)
