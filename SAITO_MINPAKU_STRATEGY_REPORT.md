## 1. MINPAKU VS. AIRBNB: CRITICAL DIFFERENCES

### Regulatory Framework:
- **Minpaku (民泊):** Licensed, regulated, 180-day annual limit
- **Unlicensed "Airbnb":** Grey area, enforcement risk, potential fines

### Key Minpaku Requirements:
1. **Fire Safety:** Sprinklers, fire alarms, evacuation plans
2. **Notification:** Must notify neighbors within 10-day window
3. **Management:** Local manager required within 1-hour response time
4. **Record Keeping:** Guest registry, cleaning logs, incident reports
5. **Insurance:** Special Minpaku liability coverage

### Why Minpaku Wins for Serious Investors:

**Advantage 1: Regulatory Certainty**
- Clear rules = predictable business environment
- Licensed properties can't be arbitrarily shut down
- Banks more willing to finance licensed operations

**Advantage 2: Reduced Competition**
- 80% of "Airbnb" listings are unlicensed (facing crackdowns)
- Minpaku license creates scarcity = premium pricing power
- Corporate players avoid complexity = less competition

**Advantage 3: Professional Positioning**
- "Fully Licensed Minpaku" commands trust from Japanese guests
- Business travelers prefer licensed accommodations
- Long-term viability in regulated market
